\subsubsection{Short Sword}\label{weapon:shortSword}
Weapon, Sword, One-Handed, Simple, Melee\\
Size: M\\
Draw/Sheath: 3AP\\
Cost: 75 Coins

\textbf{Thrust}\\
2 AP, STR or AGI to hit, Critical on 19 and 20, 1.5m Reach\\
1d8 + \sfrac{1}{3}\texttimes STR or AGI Piercing Damage

\textbf{Slash}\\
3 AP, STR or AGI to hit, Critical on 19 and 20, 1.5m Reach\\
1d6 + \sfrac{1}{3}\texttimes STR or AGI Cutting Damage

\textbf{Block}\\
5 Stamina, STR to block\\
1d4 + \sfrac{1}{3}\texttimes STR or AGI Blunt, Cutting, or Piercing Damage
