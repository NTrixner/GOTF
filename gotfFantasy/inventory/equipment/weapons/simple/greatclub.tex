\subsubsection{Great Club}\label{weapon:greatClub}
Weapon, Blunt, Two-Handed, Simple, Melee\\
Size: L\\
Draw/Sheath: None\\
Cost: 50 Gold

\textbf{Strike} \\
6 AP, STR to hit, 1.5m Reach\\
2d10 + \sfrac{1}{3}\texttimes STR Blunt Damage\\
When reducing a creature to 0 Health with this Attack, you can choose to knock them out instead of killing them.

\textbf{Block}
5 Stamina, STR to block\\
1d4 + \sfrac{1}{3}\texttimes STR Blunt, Cutting, or Piercing Damage
