\subsubsection{Lance}\label{weapon:lance}
Weapon, Polearm, One-Handed, Regular, Melee\\
Size: L\\
Draw/Sheath: None\\
Cost: 150 Coins

\textbf{Thrust}\\
4 AP, STR to hit, 3m Reach\\
2d10 + \sfrac{1}{3}\texttimes STR Piercing Damage

\textbf{Charge}\\
When performing a ~\nameref{maneuver:chargeAttack}, the Lance deals 3d6 + \sfrac{1}{3}\texttimes STR Piercing Damage instead.
