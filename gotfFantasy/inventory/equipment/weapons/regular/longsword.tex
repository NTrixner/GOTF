Size: L\\
Draw/Sheath: 5AP\\

6 AP, STR to hit, Critical on 19 and 20, 3m Reach\\
2d10 + \sfrac{1}{3}\texttimes STR Piercing Damage

5 AP, STR to hit, Critical on 19 and 20, 3m Reach\\
3d10 + \sfrac{1}{3}\texttimes STR Cutting Damage

\textbf{Block}\\
STR to block\\
2d6 + \sfrac{1}{2}\texttimes STR Blunt, Cutting, or Piercing Damage
