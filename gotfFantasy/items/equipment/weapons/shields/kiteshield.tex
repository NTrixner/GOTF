\subsubsection{Kite Shield}\label{weapon:kiteShield}
Weapon, Shield, One-Handed, Melee\\
Size: M\\
Draw/Sheath: 7AP\\
Cost: 50 Gold

\textbf{Shield Bash}\\
3AP, STR to hit, 1.5m Reach\\
1d4 + \sfrac{1}{4}\texttimes STR Blunt Damage\\
When reducing a creature to 0 Health with this Attack, you can choose to knock them out instead of killing them.

\textbf{Block}\\
STR to block\\
2d6 + \sfrac{1}{3}\texttimes STR Blunt, Cutting, or Piercing
This shield can be used to block ranged ammunition attacks.\\
