Size: M\\
Draw/Sheath: 3AP\\

5 AP, STR or AGI to hit, Critical on 19 and 20, 1.5m Reach\\
2d8 + \sfrac{1}{2}\texttimes STR or AGI Cutting Damage

\textbf{Block}\\
STR to block\\
1d6 + \sfrac{1}{3}\texttimes STR or AGI Blunt, Cutting, or Piercing Damage
