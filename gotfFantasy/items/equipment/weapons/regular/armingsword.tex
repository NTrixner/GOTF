\subsubsection{Arming Sword}\label{weapon:armingSword}
Weapon, Sword, One-Handed, Regular, Melee\\
Size: M\\
Draw/Sheath: 3AP\\
Cost: 250 Gold

\textbf{Thrust}\\
3 AP, STR or AGI to hit, Critical on 19 and 20, 1.5m Reach\\
1d8 + \sfrac{1}{3}\texttimes STR or AGI Piercing Damage

\textbf{Slash}\\
4 AP, STR or AGI to hit, Critical on 19 and 20, 1.5m Reach\\
3d6 + \sfrac{1}{3}\texttimes STR or AGI Cutting Damage

\textbf{Block}\\
STR to block\\
1d6 + \sfrac{1}{3}\texttimes STR Blunt, Cutting, or Piercing Damage
