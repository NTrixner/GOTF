\subsubsection{Club}\label{weapon:club}
Weapon, Blunt, One-Handed, Simple, Melee\\
Size: S\\
Draw/Sheath: 2AP\\
Cost: 20 Coins

\textbf{Strike}\\
3AP, STR to hit, 1.5m Reach\\
1d8 + \sfrac{1}{4}\texttimes STR Blunt Damage\\
When reducing a creature to 0 Health with this Attack, you can choose to knock them out instead of killing them.

\textbf{Block}\\
3 Stamina, STR to block\\
1d4 + \sfrac{1}{4}\texttimes STR Blunt, Cutting, or Piercing Damage
